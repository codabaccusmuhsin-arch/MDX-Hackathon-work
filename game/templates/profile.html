Spider-Man Profile

Name

Spider-Man

Identity

Unknown, but is widely suspected to be Peter Parker, a freelance photographer and science enthusiast from Queens, New York.

Notable Deeds

  • Helped 36 neighbourhood grandmas cross the road.
  • Retrieved and returned over 500 stolen bicycles to their rightful owners.
  • Regularly foils robberies, carjackings, and other petty crimes.
  • Assisted in the evacuation of countless civilians during major superhero clashes.

Known Abilities

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and agility.
  • The ability to cling to most surfaces.
  • Proficient in hand-to-hand combat and improvisation.
